About Us

Vivekanand Education Society (VES) runs 26 institutions in the vicinity of Chembur. The Society’s aim is to impart quality education to all including the economically backward classes thereby playing an important role in the progress of our country, vision of Shri Hashu Advaniji, a great social worker. All institutions are located in Kurla and Chembur, a lush green suburb convenient to travel from any part of Mumbai.· Today, VES has a vibrant community of Native students, faculty, staff, and alumni.
